About Me

Hello! I'm Bailey.

I've always been a fan of stories. For longer than I can remember, I've been fascinated by language and worldbuilding. From coming up with Dungeons and Dragons campaigns to investigating US housing data, there are interesting topics to be found everywhere.

I graduated from UCSD in 2022 with a degree in Data Science and Cognitive Science, during which I interned at InVia Robotics and the San Diego Supercomputer Center. Both positions hired me full-time after their respective internships, and I continued working at the Supercomputer Center after graduation. My next position was as a Data Scientist at Fulcra Dynamics Corporation, designing and building data infrastructure to support Digital Twins.

These days, I work on personal projects and keep pace with the rapid development of AI, and optimize the minutia of the machines I currently work with. I'm always looking for new opportunities to learn and grow, so feel free to reach out!
